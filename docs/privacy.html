MySQL Mac Client

Privacy Policy · Last updated: July 2025

Short version: MySQL Mac Client does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. All data stays on your Mac.

1. Information We Collect

MySQL Mac Client does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or telemetry data is collected
  • No usage data is sent to any external server
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No crash reports are automatically uploaded

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Mac

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Connection profiles (host, port, username, database name): stored in ~/Library/Application Support/MySQLMacClient/connections.json
  • Passwords: stored securely in the macOS Keychain — never written to disk in plaintext
  • App preferences (theme, grid settings, editor font size): stored in the app's sandbox container

3. Network Access

The app makes outgoing TCP connections only to the MySQL servers you explicitly configure. No connections are made to any servers operated by the developer.

4. Third-Party Services

MySQL Mac Client does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting frameworks.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. No information is collected from any user.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the change.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on GitHub.
